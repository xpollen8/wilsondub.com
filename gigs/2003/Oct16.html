Gigs :: 2003 :: Oct16

Venue: Cargo
Where: London England
When: Sunday, Friday 16th, 2003
Opening: Justin Robertson aka Lionrock

Notes

Finally Wilson invade the capital. We're playing at Cargo, 83 Rivington Street, Shoreditch, London EC2 on 16th October. It's a club night called "For Your Pleasure". We will be playing live at about 10:00pm. The club is supposed to have one of the best sound systems in London. Should be a top night.

I guess the club opens around 7:00pm. It's £5:00 on the door up until 8:00pm. From 8:00pm it's £8:00 on the door. If you are on our guest list, it will cost you just £5:00 whenever you bowl up.

Nearest tube station: Old Street or Liverpool Street. Check out Cargo at http://www.cargo-london.com. The Cargo phone number is 020 7739 3440.
